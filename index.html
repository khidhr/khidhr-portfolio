Problem statement:

Skin cancer is a common disease caused by abnormal skin cell growth due to DNA damage from UV radiation (sun or tanning beds). It has three main types: basal cell carcinoma (BCC), squamous cell carcinoma (SCC), and melanoma. Melanoma is the least common but the most dangerous, as it spreads rapidly and can be lethal if not detected early.

Contribution:
